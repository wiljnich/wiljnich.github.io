2018 Political Digital Advertising Report

Alongside a great team at Tech for Campaigns, I worked on the nation's most comprehensive analysis of political digital advertising during the 2018 election. This includes a complete view of American political spending on Facebook and Google in the run up to the midterms, and was featured in The New York Times, The Wall Street Journal, and Politico.

Indiana Criminal Justice Institute 2015 Report

An initial evaluation of Indiana’s Criminal Code reform submitted to the Indiana Criminal Justice Institute pursuant to HEA 1006. I compiled sections pertaining to pretrial release and improvements to data systems.


Strategies for Public Cloud Adoption and Governance at the Ministry of Justice and Security

This report was developed as part of an academic consulting engagement with the Ministry of Justice and Security through TU Delft. It evaluates governance challenges and recommends strategies for the effective adoption of a public cloud for data storage by the Ministry.

Policy Memos

Popular Science Essay - Nuclear Waste Storage at Yucca Mountain

This essay was written for a graduate course in engineering policy analysis at TU Delft to explore the technical and political elements behind the gridlocked Yucca Mountain nuclear waste storage site.

Policy Memo - Supreme Court Ruling in Kisor v. Wilkie

This memo was written for a graduate law course to detail the potential impacts of an upcoming U.S. Supreme Court ruling to a relevant interest group and recommend appropriate action.

Policy Memo - Building Consensus for a National Energy Policy

This memo was written for a graduate energy systems course and answers a request for a practical approach by which to build broad consensus for a cohesive national energy policy in 2019.

Research Paper - Automatic Continuing Resolutions and Congressional Appropriations

This research paper was written for a graduate public finance course under Robert S. Kravchuk and considers the Automatic Continuing Resolution mechanism as an element of Congressional appropriations policy, concluding that the long-term financial ramifications of these resolutions far outweigh the cost savings presented by such proposals.
