I'm a policy and data professional interested in the application of data-driven technologies to equitably solve environmental challenges at scale. At the US Environmental Protection Agency, I build and maintain data systems to support the EPA’s management of hazardous and non-hazardous solid waste under the Resource Conservation and Recovery Act. Prior to joining the EPA, I developed analytical tools for water policy research, local government procurement, and supply chain management.

I’m a graduate of the Indiana University O’Neill School of Public and Environmental Affairs, where I earned an MPA focused on resource systems analysis and a BS in public sector management & information systems.
